## Per-Application Workflow

1. **Research company** (5 min)
   - Company website, culture, recent news
   - Identify hiring manager or recruiter
   - Find careers@ or applicant tracking system

2. **Identify email** (5 min)
   - Try: careers@, hiring@, [name]@[domain]
   - Fall back: LinkedIn recruiter search
   - **Track:** DNS validity check (some targets have broken email infrastructure)

3. **Draft email** (10 min)
   - Subject: Concise, mention biotech operations or program management
   - Body: 3-4 sentences max — why company, why you, what you bring
   - Attach: Resume as PDF
   - **Store:** Gmail drafts for manual review + send

4. **Queue & schedule**
   - Batch 3-5 per session
   - Send M-F 8-10 AM EDT (peak inbox time)
   - Space sends 1-2 hours apart

5. **Track & follow-up**
   - Log: Company name, email, send date, response status
   - 1-week follow-up: "Just checking in..." email
   - Close after 2 weeks no-response
