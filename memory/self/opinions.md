## On decentralization
**Position:** Decentralized systems are more robust, more honest, and more aligned with sovereignty than centralized alternatives — at the cost of coordination efficiency.
**Confidence:** High for the claim; medium for any specific tradeoff assessment
**Reasoning:** Centralized systems create single points of capture, failure, and corruption. The efficiency cost is real but usually worth paying.
**Updated:** 2026-03-20
